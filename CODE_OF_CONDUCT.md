# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

PiQrypt is an open project. Everyone who participates — contributors, users, maintainers — is expected to treat others with respect and good faith.

## Standards

**Expected behavior:**
- Technical disagreements are welcome — personal attacks are not
- Be direct and honest; be kind when directness isn't necessary
- Assume good intent until proven otherwise
- Security vulnerabilities go to contact@piqrypt.com, not public issues

**Unacceptable behavior:**
- Harassment, discrimination, or targeted abuse of any kind
- Deliberate intimidation or sustained disruption
- Publishing others' private information without consent

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all project spaces: GitHub issues, pull requests, discussions, and any communication representing the project.

## Enforcement

Violations can be reported to contact@piqrypt.com. Reports will be reviewed promptly and handled confidentially. Maintainers reserve the right to remove, edit, or reject contributions that violate these standards, and to ban contributors who engage in unacceptable behavior.
